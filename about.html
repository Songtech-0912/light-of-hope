About

Light of Hope is an open-source initiative to produce high-quality, freely-available musical pieces. These pieces are intended to be comforting - songs you'd want to listen to on a bad day, when you're struggling or upset or stressed out. And they are meant to be fully public-domain, with no limitations on usage or distribution, to reach as many people as possible. Contributions are very much welcome: let's be a light of hope for people who need that light.
